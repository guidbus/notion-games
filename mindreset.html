1/15• 30 sec

1) Breathe Deeply

Inhale 4 • Hold 2 • Exhale 6 • Hold 2. Let your shoulders drop.

2/15• 35 sec

2) Relax Your Body

Unclench jaw, loosen neck, soften hands and belly.

3/15• 40 sec

3) Ground with Senses

Name 3 colours, 2 shapes, 1 sound around you.

4/15• 45 sec

4) Name the Feeling

Say: “I feel anxious / sad / angry / lost — and that’s okay.”

5/15• 45 sec

5) Accept the Moment

You don’t need to fix everything now. Breathe and be.

6/15• 50 sec

6) Reframe Gently

“I can handle this one small step at a time.”

7/15• 55 sec

7) Micro-Relax

Close eyes 5 sec • open slowly • soften forehead.

8/15• 60 sec

8) Hydrate or Stretch

Small sip of water or shoulder roll x5.

9/15• 60 sec

9) Tiny Win

Recall one thing you did right today (even small).

10/15• 65 sec

10) Focus Word

Choose: Calm • Strength • Hope • Light — breathe it in.

11/15• 70 sec

11) Body Scan

Notice one area that feels okay; let it spread.

12/15• 75 sec

12) Long Exhale

Exhale slightly longer than inhale for 5 breaths.

13/15• 80 sec

13) Gentle Kindness

Tell yourself: “I’m doing my best, and that is enough.”

14/15• 85 sec

14) Next Tiny Action

Decide one 2-minute task you’ll start after this.

15/15• 90 sec

15) Close with Calm

Three slow breaths and a soft smile. Carry your word.

✨ Session Complete — You are calmer and clearer.
