1/12• 30 sec

Breath & Posture

Sit tall. Inhale 4, exhale 6 for five cycles. Anchor your presence.

2/12• 40 sec

Define the Trait

Write one sentence: “Today I practice your path through small actions.”

3/12• 45 sec

One Tiny Rule

Set a 2-minute rule you can do now (tidy desk, drink water, stretch).

4/12• 60 sec

Obstacle → Plan

Note your biggest distraction. Write one line: “If it appears, I will ___.”

5/12• 45 sec

Micro Action

Do a 90-second action related to your path (e.g., send a kind message, read 1 page).

6/12• 45 sec

Identity Line

Say quietly: “I am the kind of person who keeps small promises.”

7/12• 50 sec

Gratitude x2

Name two things you’re grateful for today. Feel them for a breath.

8/12• 50 sec

Kindness Move

Do one small kind act (text, thank-you, help). Keep it simple.

9/12• 55 sec

Focus Sprint

Clear just one tab/task. 2 minutes. No perfection — just finish.

10/12• 55 sec

Resilience Reframe

Turn a setback into a lesson: “From this, I learned to ___.”

11/12• 60 sec

Future You

Picture yourself after 30 days practicing this path. What’s different?

12/12• 60 sec

Seal the Upgrade

Choose a word to carry today: Steady • Brave • Kind • Clear. Breathe it in.
